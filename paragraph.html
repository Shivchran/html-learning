Shaktimaan is India's most popular superhero, created and portrayed by Mukesh Khanna. The television series aired on Doordarshan and became a favorite among children in the late 1990s and early 2000s. Shaktimaan used his extraordinary powers to fight evil and protect people. His alter ego, Gangadhar, worked as a photographer. The show also taught moral values and safety lessons, making it both entertaining and educational.

Junior G is an Indian superhero television series that aired on Doordarshan. The story revolves around a young boy named Gaurav who gains special powers from an alien device and becomes the superhero Junior G. He uses his powers to fight criminals, help people, and protect society from evil forces. The show was popular among children for its action, adventure, and positive messages about courage and responsibility.
