Top Three Movie According to Sachin

My Best Three Movie

Dangal

Dangal is one of the most successful Indian movies of all time because it tells an emotional and inspiring story of a father who trains his daughters to become wrestlers. Aamir Khan’s powerful performance, the realistic sports scenes, and the message of women empowerment connected with audiences in India and abroad. The movie became especially popular in China because families related to its themes of hard work, parental expectations, and achieving dreams. This international success made Dangal the highest-grossing Indian film worldwide.

Baahubali 2

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion became a blockbuster because it offered a grand cinematic experience with huge sets, action scenes, visual effects, music, and a strong story. People were very curious to know the answer to the famous question, “Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?” The film had memorable characters such as Baahubali, Bhallaladeva, and Sivagami, and it was released in many languages. Its larger-than-life presentation attracted audiences across India and helped it become one of the highest-grossing Indian films.

Pushpa 2

Pushpa 2: The Rule became highly successful because audiences were already excited after the first Pushpa movie. Allu Arjun’s strong style, dialogue delivery, action scenes, and the character of Pushpa Raj became very popular among fans. The movie had mass entertainment, powerful villains, catchy music, and dramatic moments that worked well with both single-screen and multiplex audiences. Its popularity across Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, and Kannada markets helped it earn a huge worldwide collection.
